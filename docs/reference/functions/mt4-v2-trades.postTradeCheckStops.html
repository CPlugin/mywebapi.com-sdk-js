Pump-cached call. The MT4 server verifies that the supplied SL/TP (and, for pending orders, the open price) sit at least `stops_level` away from the current market and that pending expiration is at least ten minutes in the future. Returns a bare boolean envelope: `true` when the wrapper's `ResultCode` is `Ok`.
